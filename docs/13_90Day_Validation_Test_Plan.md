# EcoForge Container 1 – 90-Day Validation Test Plan

**Version**: 1.0 – March 22, 2026  
**Status**: Locked core document in Master Archive v1.6

**Objective**  
Run a 90-day continuous test on Danny’s Container 1 to prove the core loop under real conditions, collect verifiable data, and validate minimum viability for self-funding expansion. Designed for 1–2 operators.

### 1. Pre-Test Setup (Days –14 to 0)
**Required**  
- Container placed, geothermal commissioned, solar/battery live, micro-hydro connected.  
- Tilapia tank filled & biofloc inoculated.  
- Vermi beds stocked (10–15 lb red wigglers).  
- Duckweed trays seeded.  
- Mars bay attached, LEDs installed, low-pressure chamber mock sealed.  
- All probes calibrated.

**Go/No-Go**  
If ammonia >1 ppm or DO <4 mg/L by Day –7, delay and troubleshoot.

### 2. Test Phases

**Phase 1 – Stabilization (Days 1–30)**  
**Daily**  
- Temp 78–86°F, DO >5 mg/L, pH 6.8–7.5, ammonia <0.5 ppm, nitrite <1 ppm  
- Feed tilapia 1–3% body weight  
- Harvest 20–30% duckweed  

**Weekly**  
- Brew/dilute worm tea  
- Clean swirl settler  
- Check biofloc TSS  

**Day 30 Success**  
- Ammonia <0.5 ppm average  
- No fish mortality >5%  
- Duckweed doubling observed  

**Phase 2 – Steady Operation (Days 31–60)**  
**Daily**  
- Log harvest weights and fish behavior  

**Weekly**  
- Weigh tilapia (track FCR)  
- Measure floc protein  
- 24-hour low-pressure test  

**Day 60 Success**  
- Ammonia removal >70% average  
- Tilapia FCR <2.0  
- No system crash  

**Phase 3 – Stress & Closure (Days 61–90)**  
**Daily**  
- Simulate 1 controlled stress/week (pump off 24h, pH shift ±0.5, temp drop to 70°F)  

**Weekly**  
- 7-day sealed Mars bay run  
- Full water & nitrogen balance  

**Day 90 Success**  
- Survive 2–3 stress events  
- Water recovery >60%  
- Nutrient cycling >60%  
- Positive net balance

### 3. Monitoring & Data
**Daily Log**  
- All parameters (pH, DO, temp, ammonia, nitrate, EC, TSS)  
- Harvest weights  
- Feed input  
- Fish behavior  
- Biofloc visual  
- Energy charge  

**Weekly Summary**  
- Avg/max values  
- FCR  
- Closure estimates  

**Failure Modes**  
- Ammonia spike → carbon dose + exchange  
- Low DO → add air stones  
- Fish loss >5% → salt bath, isolate  

### 4. Success Thresholds (Day 90)
**Minimum Viable**  
- No total crash  
- Ammonia <0.5 ppm average last 30 days  
- Tilapia survival >85%  
- Duckweed doubling  
- Greens >500 lb total  
- Water recovery >60%  

**Strong Outcome**  
- Ammonia removal >80%  
- Tilapia FCR <1.8  
- Greens >800 lb  
- Water recovery >70%
